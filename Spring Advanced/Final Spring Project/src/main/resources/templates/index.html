Amenities

  • Spa Areas

    Our spa department offers a one-of-a-kind and unforgettable experience. the innovative smart jacuzzi for 10 people opens up all your senses with its massaging nozzles. The automated temperature control makes it possible to adjust it to that of the body so that you feel as if there is no water. Sauna and steam bath also for 10 people is another asset that will add to your amazing spa experience.

  • Fitness Zones

    Тhe fitness area has absolutely all the equipment with the help of which all muscle groups in the human body are trained. For your comfort, the fitness area is divided into different rooms with different functionality. A room for group cycling, a room for yoga classes, a main room with fitness equipment as well as changing rooms and showers, equipped with the necessary toiletries that one needs.

  • Swimming Pools

    Two types of pools provided for our club members. An outdoor pool located on the roof of the building with a breathtaking 360 degree view. An indoor pool, which has a specialized system through which the temperature in the room as well as in the pool matches the body temperature. Special panels imitating sunlight and reproducing ultraviolet rays contribute to feeling as if you are sitting outdoors under the sun.

  • Solarium

    The solarium provides the opportunity to build an evenly distributed tan over the entire body. With its special systems, the risk of producing more ultraviolet rays than necessary is minimized to zero. all control is put into a touch control device. It is also possible to connect your mobile smart device via bluetooth and control not only all functions, but also play music through the built-in speakers.

  • Parking

    We understand the difficulty of parking in big cities. All our clubs have parking for a total of 100 cars, divided into outdoor parking and indoor underground parking. In addition, the centers have an innovative and ultra-modern self-service car wash equipped with everything your car needs. For all club members, the parking lot as well as the car wash can be used completely free of charge.

  • Fresh Bar

    What's better than a healthy drink after a workout? Enjoy and immerse yourself in the world of healthy food and drinks in our fresh bar. It has a wide variety of fruits and vegetables to make smoothies and fresh, as well as all kinds of healthy seeds and nuts. Healthy desserts are another addition that you have the opportunity to try. Better come and try it for yourself to experience the amazing flavors and quality of all our products.
